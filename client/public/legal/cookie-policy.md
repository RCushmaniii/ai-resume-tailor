## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit and provide functionality.

---

## Our Cookie Philosophy

**Privacy by design:** We minimize cookie usage to only what's essential for service operation.

**No tracking cookies:** We don't use cookies for advertising, user tracking, or behavioral analysis.

**Transparency first:** This policy explains exactly what cookies we use and why.

---

## Types of Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies

**Purpose:** Required for basic website functionality and security.

**What they do:**

- Maintain secure connections between your browser and our servers
- Enable HTTPS security protocols
- Remember your language preference (English/Spanish)
- Support website functionality during your session

**Duration:** Session-based (deleted when you close your browser)

**Can you disable them?** No - these are required for the website to function properly.

---

### Analytics Cookies

**Purpose:** Help us understand how people use our service to improve it.

**What they do:**

- Count total visitors (anonymized, no personal data)
- Measure which features are most used
- Identify technical issues or broken functionality
- Track overall site performance and uptime

**Duration:** 30 days

**Can you disable them?** Yes - you can opt out of analytics without affecting service functionality.

**Important:** We never connect analytics data to your resume content or personal information.

---

### No Advertising or Commercial Tracking

**We do NOT use:**

- Advertising cookies
- Social media tracking cookies
- Cross-site retargeting cookies
- Personalization cookies
- Commercial data collection

**Why not?** Our privacy-first design means we don't collect the data needed for these commercial purposes.

## Managing Your Cookie Preferences

### Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

**Chrome:**

1. Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
2. Choose your preferred cookie settings

**Firefox:**

1. Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
2. Manage your cookie preferences

**Safari:**

1. Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
2. Configure your cookie settings

**Edge:**

1. Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Cookies
2. Choose your cookie preferences

### Opt-Out Options

**Analytics Opt-Out:**

- Install the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
- Use your browser's "Do Not Track" setting
- Disable cookies entirely in your browser settings

**Impact of Opt-Out:**

- Service functionality remains unchanged
- Resume analysis still works perfectly
- No personal features are lost
